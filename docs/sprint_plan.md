# Sprint Plan

## Sprint 1 - Pandas Fundamentals
Focus: loading data, inspecting datasets, filtering, creating columns, grouping and exporting CSV files.

## Sprint 2 - Data Cleaning
Focus: missing values, duplicates, invalid keys, type conversion and date parsing.

## Sprint 3 - Joins and Business Reporting
Focus: merging multiple datasets, building business-level reports and validating results.

## Sprint 4 - KPIs and Performance Analysis
Focus: revenue, margin, average order value, product performance and store performance.

## Sprint 5 - Advanced Analysis
Focus: returns analysis, inventory risk, marketing campaign impact and final executive summary.
